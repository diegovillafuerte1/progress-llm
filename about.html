About Progress Knight

What is Progress Knight?

Progress Knight is an incremental idle game where you progress through various jobs and skills, building your character's wealth and abilities over time. The game runs in your browser and continues to progress even when you're not actively playing.

Game Features

  • Idle Gameplay: Your character continues to work and gain experience even when you're away
  • Multiple Jobs: Progress through different career paths from beggar to legendary knight
  • Skill System: Develop various skills that enhance your character's abilities
  • Shop System: Purchase items and properties to boost your progress
  • Rebirth System: Start over with permanent bonuses for faster progression
  • Ad Rewards: Watch ads or click sponsored content for in-game bonuses

How to Play

  1. Choose a job to work on (start with Beggar)
  2. Watch your character gain experience and level up
  3. Unlock new jobs and skills as you progress
  4. Purchase items and properties to boost your income
  5. Use the rebirth system to start over with permanent bonuses

Advertisements

Progress Knight is supported by advertisements. You can:

  • Click on sponsored content for immediate rewards
  • Watch video ads for temporary bonuses
  • Support the game by engaging with relevant advertisements

Technical Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • Local storage enabled (for saving game progress)

Data Privacy

Your game data is stored locally in your browser. We do not collect personal information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
